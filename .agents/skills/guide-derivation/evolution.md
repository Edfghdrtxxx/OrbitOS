## 2026-03-10
### Lessons
- Record in-session Q&A (motivation, meaning of terms, why a step is needed) as `> [!faq]` callouts in the derivation note so they persist for future review — don't leave them in terminal output.
- **Dual-explanation mode (default):** Every derivation step and FAQ gets **two layers**: (1) academic/rigorous explanation, (2) Feynman-style analogy in plain language. Both go into the note — academic ensures exam-readiness, Feynman ensures genuine understanding. Applies to both quick and learn modes.
