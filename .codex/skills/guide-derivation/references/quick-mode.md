# Quick Mode — Full Derivation

One-click completion. Produce the full derivation and save it.

## Q1. Clarify the Target

Confirm what exactly to derive and from which starting point. If ambiguous, ask.

## Q2. State the Setup

List the premises, known results, and the strategy in one short block.

## Q3. Derive Step by Step

Each step states what we do, why, and the math. Rules:
- Name every identity or theorem used.
- Flag every approximation and non-trivial assumption explicitly.
- After key steps, briefly say what it means physically or geometrically.

## Q4. Box the Result

Final expression in `\boxed{}`, followed by:
- **Valid when:** conditions / domain of validity
- **Breaks when:** where assumptions fail

## Q5. Highlight Pitfalls

1–3 common errors or exam traps for this derivation.

## Q6. Save

Save the derivation per the Output section in `SKILL.md`.
