# Certificate of Eligibility (COE)
## Definition
A pre-screening document issued by Japanese immigration authorities to confirm that an applicant meets the conditions for a specific status of residence before visa issuance.

## Key Points
- COE is commonly used for both work-track and student-track long-term stays.
- The receiving organization in Japan (employer or university) usually applies for it.
- Visa issuance at an embassy/consulate still requires mission-specific checks and documents.

## Examples
- Work track: sponsoring company applies for COE, then the applicant applies for visa.
- Student track: university applies for COE, then the applicant applies for visa.
