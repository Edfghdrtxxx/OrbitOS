# Engineer / Specialist in Humanities / International Services
## Definition
A major Japanese work status of residence category for professional and technical roles, often used by international graduates entering Japan-based employment.

## Key Points
- Eligibility is based on legal criteria for activities and qualifications, not only a single degree checkbox.
- Applicant background and planned duties must align with the authorized activity scope.
- Sponsoring employer involvement is central in the COE and visa process.

## Examples
- ML engineer hired by a Japanese company under engineer-related professional duties.
- International business role under specialist in humanities/international services criteria.
