# Student Visa (Japan)
## Definition
A status-of-residence pathway used for formal study at Japanese educational institutions, typically initiated through school acceptance and COE support.

## Key Points
- University acceptance and proof of financial support are core requirements.
- The school generally coordinates COE application support.
- Post-graduation transition to other statuses is possible under defined rules.

## Examples
- Graduate student enters Japan with university support, then transitions to job-hunting designated activities or work status.
