# Mission-Specific Visa Document Requirements
## Definition
The principle that final visa application document requirements are determined by the Japanese embassy/consulate serving the applicant's residence, even when general MOFA guidance exists.

## Key Points
- Core process information is published by MOFA, but required attachments can vary by mission and nationality.
- Photo format, passport validity handling, and supplementary evidence often differ in practical checklists.
- Applicants should confirm latest requirements immediately before submission.

## Examples
- Two applicants with similar profiles may submit different attachment sets depending on consular jurisdiction.
