# Designated Activities for Job Hunting (Tokutei Katsudo)
## Definition
A post-graduation status pathway that can allow eligible foreign graduates in Japan to continue job hunting after completing studies.

## Key Points
- This is a specific designated-activities route, not the same as standard student status.
- Eligibility conditions and permitted stay period are rule-bound and should be checked in current ISA guidance.
- Typical use is as a bridge between graduation and transition to a work status.

## Examples
- Graduate finishes degree, receives designated activities permission for continued job hunting, then changes to a work status after offer.
