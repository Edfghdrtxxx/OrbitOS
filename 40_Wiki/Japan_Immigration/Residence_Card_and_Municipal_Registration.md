# Residence Card and Municipal Registration
## Definition
The post-arrival administrative sequence for mid-to-long-term residents in Japan, combining residence card handling and local address registration obligations.

## Key Points
- Residence card handling begins at entry under immigration procedures.
- Municipal address registration is required after securing a local address.
- Insurance and banking setup typically follow resident registration.

## Examples
- New worker enters Japan, obtains/updates residence card status, registers address at city office, then proceeds with insurance and bank account setup.
