# Japan Graduate Scholarships: MEXT and JASSO
## Definition
Two major scholarship channels relevant to international students in Japan: MEXT (government scholarship system) and JASSO-administered student support programs.

## Key Points
- MEXT and JASSO are distinct mechanisms with different eligibility, selection flows, and funding conditions.
- Scholarship planning should be synchronized with university application timelines.
- Program details and yearly conditions should be re-checked on official pages before application.

## Examples
- Applicant targets MEXT through embassy/university channels while also tracking JASSO and university fellowship options.
