# Highly Skilled Professional (HSP) System
## Definition
A points-based status framework for highly skilled foreign professionals in Japan, with policy advantages that can include accelerated permanent residency pathways.

## Key Points
- Points are evaluated using structured criteria published by immigration authorities.
- Route selection and points strategy should be planned early for long-term residency goals.
- PR acceleration is conditional and tied to meeting program requirements.

## Examples
- A professional with qualifying education, income, and achievements applies through an HSP track and later pursues PR under accelerated conditions.
