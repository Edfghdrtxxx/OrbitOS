# Institute of Modern Physics

## Definition

The Institute of Modern Physics (IMP) is a research institute under the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), active in nuclear and particle physics.

## Key Points

- IMP appears in RIKEN IPA partner listings as part of `Chinese Academy of Science (IMP, XIOPM)`.
- For IPA communication, using `IMP, CAS` is clearer than `UCAS` alone.
- IMP administrative approval is required for long-term overseas doctoral research arrangements.

## Examples

Using affiliation in emails: `Institute of Modern Physics (IMP), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS)`.
