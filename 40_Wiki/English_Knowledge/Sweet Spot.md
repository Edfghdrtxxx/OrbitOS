# Sweet Spot

## Definition

The **sweet spot** is the optimal point or ideal condition where something works best — the perfect balance between competing factors. The collocation is always *sweet **spot***, never "sweet point."

## Key Points

- Originally from sports (the area on a bat or racket that produces the best hit), now used broadly in business, engineering, and everyday English
- Always a noun phrase: "find the sweet spot," "hit the sweet spot"
- Common collocations: *find / hit / reach / be in* the sweet spot
- The word **spot** pairs with *sweet*; swapping to "point" breaks the [[Collocation]]

## Examples

- "The price is in the **sweet spot** — affordable but still profitable."
- "We need to find the **sweet spot** between speed and accuracy."
