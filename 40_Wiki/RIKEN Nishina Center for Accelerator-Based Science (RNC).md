# RIKEN Nishina Center for Accelerator-Based Science (RNC)

## Definition

The RIKEN Nishina Center for Accelerator-Based Science (RNC) is a major RIKEN research center focused on accelerator-based nuclear and particle science.

## Key Points

- RNC is a high-priority target for nuclear/particle-physics-aligned PhD pathways in Japan.
- It has historical collaboration ties with institutions in China, including IMP-linked research exchanges.
- PI contact and research-fit alignment are critical first steps for IPA-style pathways.

## Examples

Candidate outreach strategy: shortlist 3-5 RNC PIs whose topics match TPC/direct-reactions/ML-supported analysis.
