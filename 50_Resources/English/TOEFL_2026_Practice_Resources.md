# TOEFL iBT 2026 Practice Resources

> [!warning] New format launched Jan 21, 2026
> The TOEFL changed fundamentally. Old prep materials (pre-2026) cover **different task types** and are only useful for general skill-building. Use 2026-specific materials for test strategy and practice.

## What Changed (Quick Reference)

| Aspect | Old Format | New Format (Jan 2026+) |
|---|---|---|
| **Duration** | ~2 hours | ~1.5 hours |
| **Scoring** | 0-120 (30 per section) | 1-6 band (half-point increments); 0-120 comparable score provided during transition (2026-2028) |
| **Reading** | Academic passages only | Academic + daily-life (emails, articles, menus) + "Complete the Words" (cloze) |
| **Listening** | Lectures + campus conversations | Lectures + daily-life conversations; adaptive difficulty |
| **Speaking** | 4 tasks (1 independent + 3 integrated) | 2 tasks: **Listen and Repeat** + **Take an Interview** |
| **Writing** | Integrated essay (20 min) + Academic Discussion (10 min) | **Write an Email** + **Write for an Academic Discussion** (no more integrated essay) |
| **Adaptive** | No | Yes — Reading and Listening adapt based on section performance |
| **Question style** | Longer, fewer items | Shorter, more interactive "items" (up to 50 R / 47 L / 11 S / 12 W) |

> [!tip] Score equivalence for your target
> Your target of 100/120 corresponds roughly to band **5.0** on the new 1-6 scale. Confirm with ETS's concordance table closer to your exam date.

## Recommended Practice Sequence

1. **Now:** Take the free ETS Sample Test to get a 2026-format baseline
2. **Daily:** Use TOEFL Resources free questions (rotate Writing emails, Discussion prompts, Speaking interview topics)
3. **Weekly:** One timed section from Magoosh free test or BestMyTest
4. **Monthly:** Full timed mock under real conditions (ETS or Magoosh)
5. **If budget allows:** Magoosh paid ($100/6mo) gives the most 2026-format volume with ETS-licensed questions
