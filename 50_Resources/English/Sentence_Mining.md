# Sentence Mining

Collect one well-written sentence per day while reading abstracts. Extract the structural template, then rewrite it with your own content. After 20-30 templates, academic patterns become automatic. (See [[B2_to_C1_English_Methods#4. Sentence Mining from Papers]])

## How to Add

1. Paste the sentence under the right category (or **Uncategorized**)
2. Extract the **template** — replace content words with `[placeholders]`
3. Write your own version using the template (optional but recommended)

**Format:**

> "Original sentence." — *Author, Year*
> **Template:** `[Subject] + verb + [content]...`
> **Mine:** "Your version here."
