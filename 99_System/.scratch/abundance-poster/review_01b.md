### NEW FINDING 3.2 -- Timescale "~0.1-10 Gyr" for AGB s-process is broad (INFORMATIONAL)

**Location:** Block 2.1, Stage 4, bullet 2
**Observation:** The s-process timescale is given as "~0.1-10 Gyr." This spans the full range of AGB star lifetimes (from ~8 M_sun at ~0.03 Gyr to ~1 M_sun at ~10 Gyr), but the s-process is most efficient in the 1-4 M_sun range (timescales ~0.3-3 Gyr). The stated range is not wrong but is very inclusive. For a poster this level of precision is acceptable. **No action required.**
