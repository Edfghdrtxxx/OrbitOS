## Applicant Context

- Applicant background: Master's student at [[Institute of Modern Physics]] (IMP), CAS
- Target: Doctoral research at RIKEN via IPA pathway

## 1) Eligibility Verification

### Status: `Confirmed (official page retrieved in-session on 2026-02-14)`

- RIKEN IPA page explicitly lists:
  - `Chinese Academy of Science (IMP, XIOPM)` under overseas partner institutions.
- IPA definition confirms non-Japanese doctoral candidates from partner graduate schools/institutions can apply.

### Practical implication

- Your safest naming in communication is:
  - `Institute of Modern Physics (IMP), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS)`
- This aligns directly with the wording on the IPA partner list and reduces screening confusion.

## 2) Funding / Compensation Verification

### Status: `Partially confirmed (official page retrieved in-session, interpretation corrected)`

- Official IPA compensation (RIKEN page):
  - Airfare: `1 round-trip ticket`
  - Living allowance: `5,200 JPY/day`
  - Housing: free on-campus OR off-campus rent support up to `70,000 JPY/month`
  - Accident insurance premium covered
- RIKEN wording does **not** say "180,000-200,000 JPY monthly stipend."
  - Reference conversion from official daily allowance:
    - 30-day month: `156,000 JPY`
    - 31-day month: `161,200 JPY`
- RIKEN wording does **not** explicitly state "1 round-trip per year"; it states `1 round-trip ticket` (interpret conservatively until RPAS confirms cycle policy).

### Pending confirmation with RPAS / PI

- Whether any additional monthly support exists in your specific IMP-RIKEN arrangement.
- Whether airfare is one-time over the full IPA period or renewable by year.
- Tax treatment of IPA allowance for your case (do not assume tax-free without direct confirmation).

## 3) Risk Management ("Latent Defects")

### A. Naming Trap

- Risk: Using only `UCAS` may not match IPA partner-list wording.
- Action:
  - Use `IMP, CAS` in first-line affiliation.
  - Add UCAS degree context as second-line explanatory info when needed.

### B. Agreement Validity Cycle

- Risk: Partnership agreements may be in renewal/administrative update cycles.
- Action:
  - Ask PI to confirm with IPA office (`ipa-info@ml.riken.jp`) that IMP-linked IPA intake is active for your target intake window.

### C. Home Institute Approval

- Risk: IPA requires ongoing doctoral enrollment and home-institution coordination.
- Action:
  - Confirm with IMP Education Office that long-term research at RIKEN can be recognized toward graduation requirements.

## 4) Recommended Next Steps

1. Identify 3-5 potential PIs, prioritizing [[RIKEN Nishina Center for Accelerator-Based Science (RNC)]].
2. Send first-contact emails with CV and concise fit statement (TPC/direct reactions/ML-PID).
3. Include one sentence: `I am affiliated with IMP, CAS, listed as an IPA partner institution.`
4. Ask PI + IPA office to confirm:
   - Active partnership status for your intended intake
   - Compensation interpretation (airfare cycle and overlap rules)
   - Required document flow for overseas applicants
5. Confirm IMP-side outbound research approval and credit recognition.
