## TOEFL / Language Proficiency Requirements

### What is required:
- **English proficiency:** B2 or higher (CEFR), demonstrated via TOEFL iBT, IELTS, or equivalent
- **Validity:** TOEFL scores taken within 2 years of the application start date are valid. For a ~Oct 2026 application, scores from Oct 2024 onward would be valid.
- **TOEFL scores must be submitted WITH the application** — they are part of the document package uploaded at the internal deadline.

### Is a Sep-Oct 2026 TOEFL date too late?

**It depends on the exact internal deadline, but it is risky:**

- If UTokyo's internal deadline falls around **Oct 15-31, 2026** (as it did for FY2026), then a TOEFL taken in September 2026 could work IF scores arrive in time. ETS typically delivers TOEFL iBT scores within 6-10 days.
- However, if you take the test in **late October 2026**, you risk scores arriving after the deadline.
- A **July-August 2026** TOEFL date would be much safer.
- **Recommendation: Take TOEFL by August 2026 at the latest.** September is a fallback. October is too risky.

> [!danger] TOEFL scores are required AT submission time. You cannot submit the application first and provide TOEFL later. This is confirmed by the Graduate School of Science scholarship page.

## Eligibility Confirmation

From the Graduate School of Science scholarship page:
- **Target:** "International students with outstanding academic records who wish to enter Japan in October [year] as a new student"
- **GPA requirement:** Final GPA of 2.30 or higher (on a 3.0 scale)
- **Age limit:** Born on or after April 2, 1991 (adjusts yearly)
- **Restriction:** Applicants must NOT be residing in Japan at the time of application
- **Key:** You must have a "strong recommendation from a professor of the University of Tokyo"

## Important Notes

### Quota is extremely limited
> [!warning] The Graduate School of Science can nominate **at most one applicant** per cycle to UTokyo, and UTokyo then selects from all graduate school nominees to forward to MEXT. Competition is intense.

### Kenkyusei duration under MEXT
- Research students enrolled in October have **up to 1.5 years** to pass entrance exams and matriculate to a degree program
- If you fail to pass within that window, MEXT funding ends and you must return home

### This is NOT Embassy Recommendation
- Embassy Recommendation: apply through Japanese embassy in China → MEXT screens first → university accepts
- University Recommendation: apply to UTokyo directly → UTokyo nominates to MEXT → MEXT approves
- The University Recommendation route does NOT go through the embassy at all

### The Chinese student page describes a DIFFERENT route
The page at `k.u-tokyo.ac.jp` for Chinese students describes a process involving CSC (China Scholarship Council) cooperation, which is a separate arrangement. The pure University Recommendation route does not involve CSC.
