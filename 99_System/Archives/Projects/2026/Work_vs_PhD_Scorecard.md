## Shinsotsu vs 中途採用 Evidence Note

> [!info] Added 2026-02-15. Sources: [Study in Japan](https://www.studyinjapan.go.jp/en/work-in-japan/employment/recruitment.html), [Japan Dev](https://japan-dev.com/blog/new-graduate-recruitment-japan-shinsotsu), [TokyoDev](https://www.tokyodev.com/articles/getting-a-job-in-japan-as-a-new-grad), [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simultaneous_recruiting_of_new_graduates).

**Shinsotsu (新卒採用) timeline for March 2027 graduates:**
- Internships: Summer 2025 onwards (early screening)
- 説明会 (info sessions): March 2026 (official start)
- Selection & interviews: June 2026 onwards
- 内定 (formal offers): October 2026
- Entry date: April 2027 (all new hires start together)

**Key finding — applicability to user's situation:**
- Shinsotsu is designed for 留学生 (international students enrolled at Japanese universities), not overseas graduates.
- User graduates from UCAS (China) in June 2027 → effectively excluded from shinsotsu since it requires in-person 説明会, written exams (math/Japanese/English), and multiple on-site interview rounds.
- PhD at a Japanese university would grant 留学生 status → full shinsotsu access (career fairs, university career center, on-campus recruiting, 説明会).

**中途採用 (mid-career hiring) as Work-track alternative:**
- No rigid timeline — companies hire year-round.
- Skills-based evaluation, not "potentiality hiring."
- More English-friendly, especially in tech sector.
- This is the channel most foreign engineers actually use to enter Japan.
- Foreign IT personnel grew 56% (vs 20% Japanese) between 2008-2013; trend accelerating.

**Impact on scores:**
- Work Execution Risk: unchanged (3/0.82). 中途採用 viability already partially priced in; shinsotsu exclusion is a new risk but offset by 中途採用 availability.
- PhD Execution Risk: unchanged (2/0.78). Shinsotsu access is a new positive but doesn't change the core PhD execution complexity (funding, admissions, 3-5 year commitment).
- Scores not changed numerically because the finding cuts both ways — it adds a structural advantage to PhD (shinsotsu access) while confirming a viable alternative for Work (中途採用). Net effect on gap: approximately neutral. The finding is logged for future scoring revisions.
