## Original Notes (from UTokyo_RIKEN)

**Option A: MEXT Scholarship (Embassy Recommendation via CSC)**
- Category: "Research Student" for Oct 2027 arrival
- China-specific pathway: Chinese nationals apply through the China Scholarship Council (CSC), not directly at the Japanese Embassy. CSC handles recruitment and first-round screening on behalf of the Chinese Ministry of Education.
- 2026 cycle reference (for Oct 2026 departures): application window was May 10-20, 2025 via CSC platform (sa.csc.edu.cn). Universities had internal deadlines about 1 day earlier.
- 2027 cycle (for Oct 2027 departure): expected to open around May 2026 based on annual pattern.
- Quota: about 110 slots nationwide (master's + doctoral combined) - competitive.
- Covers: tuition, living stipend, airfare.
- Requires institutional nomination: apply through UCAS/IMP graduate office. Check if IMP participates in this program.
- Status at the time: timeline confirmed as non-urgent (Feb 2026). Next action was to verify IMP participation and watch for 2027 cycle announcement around Apr-May 2026.

**Risk Note (former Risk #9)**
- Risk: Chinese nationals apply for MEXT via CSC, not directly at the Japanese Embassy. The 2026 CSC-MEXT cycle (for Oct 2026 departures) had a deadline of May 10-20, 2025. The 2027 cycle (for Oct 2027 departure) was expected around May 2026 and not yet open as of 2026-02-15. However, CSC requires institutional nomination through the university, so if IMP/UCAS does not participate, this pathway is blocked regardless of timing.
- Mitigation: Confirm IMP participation in CSC-MEXT. If IMP does not participate, self-funding is the only Phase 1 option. Quota about 110 slots nationally.
- Severity: Medium (timeline risk resolved; institutional participation was the remaining unknown).
