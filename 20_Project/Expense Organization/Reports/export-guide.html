§ G U I D E

Export Guide / 导出指南

● 支付宝 · 微信支付
for the ledger dashboard
§ 00 Overview

What you'll do

Request two bill exports — one from Alipay (支付宝), one from WeChat Pay (微信支付) — receive encrypted ZIP files by email, unzip them, and drop the CSVs into this skill's input/ folder. The dashboard ingests both formats automatically.

Each platform limits one request to a different time window: Alipay allows up to 1 year, WeChat Pay only 3 months. For a longer history, repeat the request with adjacent date ranges.

你要做什么

分别从支付宝和微信支付各申请一次账单导出，收到邮件中的加密 ZIP 后解压，把 CSV 文件放入本技能的 input/ 文件夹。仪表板会自动识别两种格式。

两个平台单次可申请的时间范围不同：支付宝 最长 1 年，微信支付 最长 3 个月。如需更长时间跨度，请按时间段分多次申请。

§ 01 Alipay · 支付宝
English
  1. Open the Alipay app. Tap the "我的" (Me) tab in the bottom navigation.
  2. Tap "账单" (Bills).
  3. Tap the "⋯" (more) icon at the top-right corner.
  4. Tap "开具交易流水证明" (Issue Transaction Flow Certificate).
  5. For Application purpose, choose "用于个人对账" (For personal reconciliation).
    Important: this option produces the CSV. The other option ("用做证明材料") produces a sealed PDF that the dashboard can't read.
  6. Enter your payment password when prompted.
  7. Configure the export:
    • Transaction type: "全部交易" (All transactions)
    • Date range: up to 1 year per request
    • Check "展示交易对手信息" (show counterparty info)
    • Check "展示商品说明信息" (show item description)
    • Tap "下一步" (Next)
  8. Enter your email address and tap "发送" (Send).
  9. Once the email arrives, open the Alipay app and go to "申请记录" (Application records) to view the unzip password.
On the unzip password: the authoritative source is the "申请记录" view inside the app. Secondary guides claim the password is the last 6 digits of the applicant's ID number, but Alipay's official docs do not confirm this — always read the in-app record.

  3. 点击右上角的 "⋯" 更多按钮。
  4. 点击 "开具交易流水证明"。
  5. "申请用途" 选择 "用于个人对账"。
    注意：此选项才会生成 CSV 文件；另一个选项 "用做证明材料" 生成的是带公章的 PDF，仪表板无法读取。

§ 02 WeChat Pay · 微信支付
English

WeChat's menu layout differs between versions. Try Path A first; if you don't see "常见问题", fall back to Path B.

Path A · Official Tencent help (current)
  1. Open WeChat. Tap the "我" (Me) tab.
  2. Tap "服务" (Services).
  3. Tap "钱包" (Wallet).
  4. Tap "账单" (Bills) in the top-right.
  5. Tap "常见问题" (FAQ) in the top-right.
  6. Tap "下载账单" (Download Bill).
Path B · Newer builds (fallback)
  • 我 ❯ 服务 ❯ 客服中心 (Customer Service Center) ❯ 下载账单
Both paths converge
  1. Purpose: choose "用于个人对账" (For personal reconciliation).
    Important: the other option ("用做证明材料") produces a PDF with company seal — not usable here.
  2. Tap "自定义时间" (Custom time). Maximum interval is 3 months per request.
  3. Enter your email, tap "下一步" (Next), then "确认邮箱" (Confirm email).
  4. Enter your payment password.
  5. WeChat emails the encrypted ZIP within 24 hours.
    The unzip password is delivered separately — as a service notification from the "微信支付" official account, inside WeChat. Check there, not in your email.
中文

微信不同版本菜单结构略有差异。优先尝试 路径 A；若看不到 "常见问题"，改走 路径 B。

路径 A · 腾讯官方客服（当前版本）
  1. 打开 微信，点击 "我"。
  2. 点击 "服务"。
  3. 点击 "钱包"。
  4. 点击右上方 "账单"。
  5. 点击右上角 "常见问题"。
  6. 点击 "下载账单"。
路径 B · 新版微信（备选）
  • 我 ❯ 服务 ❯ 客服中心 ❯ 下载账单
两条路径汇合后
  1. 用途选择 "用于个人对账"。
    注意：另一个选项 "用做证明材料" 生成的是带公章的 PDF，这里用不了。
  2. 点击 "自定义时间"，单次最长 3 个月。
  3. 填写 邮箱，点击 "下一步"，再点击 "确认邮箱"。
  4. 输入 支付密码。
  5. 微信会在 24 小时内把加密 ZIP 发到你的邮箱。
    解压密码是单独推送的 — 由微信内 "微信支付" 公众号通过服务通知发送。请在微信里查看，不在邮件中。
