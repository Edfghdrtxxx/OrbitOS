# Information of Kentaro Yako
## Quick Facts
- Position: Associate Professor, Center for Nuclear Study (CNS), Graduate School of Science, The University of Tokyo.[^yako-utokyo-en]
- Email: yako@cns.s.u-tokyo.ac.jp.[^yako-utokyo-ja]
- Lab/Group: Yako Lab (Exotic Nuclear Reaction group) under CNS graduate education listings.[^cns-graduate]
- Primary facilities referenced in his profile: SHARAQ spectrometer at RIKEN RIBF; active-target projects at HIMAC.[^yako-utokyo-ja]
- CNS operates primarily at the RIKEN Wako campus and collaborates with RIKEN Nishina Center.[^cns-overview]

## Research Focus (public descriptions)
- Experimental nuclear physics using intermediate-energy ion beams to study nuclear structure and giant resonances via nuclear scattering.[^yako-utokyo-ja]
- Current focus includes measurements with unstable RI beams at RIBF using the CNS-installed SHARAQ spectrometer, plus active-target projects at HIMAC.[^yako-utokyo-ja]
- The Exotic Nuclear Reaction group studies responses of exotic nuclei (spin/isospin responses) using unstable beams and specialized detectors (e.g., neutron detector arrays).[^cns-graduate]
