# GRE Physics Prep

> [!info] Personal prep reference for the GRE Physics Subject Test. For exam format, topic breakdown, and general facts see [[GRE Physics]]. For scheduling and deadlines see [[Official_Deadlines#3. GRE Physics Subject Test]].

## Why It Matters

GRE Physics is **compulsory** for the GSGC (chosen route) and Special Selection pathways at UTokyo Faculty of Science, Physics department. See [[Questions_and_Verifiable_Source#1. GRE Physics — Confirmed for Special Selection; Likely for GSGC]].

- UTokyo institution code: **7048**
- UTokyo requires scores **within 2 years** of application start date (stricter than ETS's 5-year validity)
- **Target window: Sep/Oct 2026** — scores arrive well before estimated GSGC Application Period 2 (~Dec 2026)

## Study Plan

### Resources

- **Primary textbook:** *Conquering the GRE Physics*
- **Supplementary:** Past ETS practice tests (available on ETS website)
- **Overlap:** Physics fundamentals track in [[Fundamental_Knowledge]] covers most GRE content areas — coordinate study to avoid duplication

### High-Yield Focus Areas

Based on weight and common pitfalls:

| Priority | Topics | Weight | Notes |
|----------|--------|--------|-------|
| High | Classical Mechanics | 20% | Largest single block; Lagrangian/Hamiltonian often tested |
| High | Electromagnetism | 18% | Maxwell's equations, boundary conditions |
| High | Quantum Mechanics + Atomic | 23% | Combined largest block; subscored separately |
| Medium | Thermo & Stat Mech | 10% | Partition functions, ensembles |
| Medium | Specialized Topics | 9% | Nuclear/particle, condensed matter — **commonly neglected, do NOT skip** |
| Medium | Optics & Waves | 8% | Interference, diffraction patterns |
| Lower | Special Relativity | 6% | Lorentz transforms, four-vectors |
| Lower | Lab Methods | 6% | Error analysis, instrumentation basics |

> [!warning] Specialized Topics pitfall
> Multiple test-takers report losing easy points by skipping review of nuclear/particle physics, condensed matter, and astrophysics "common knowledge." These 9% are essentially free points for a nuclear physics student — review them before the exam.

## Registration Checklist

- [ ] Monitor NEEA portal (`gre-main.neea.cn`) for 2026-27 cycle opening (~Jul 2026)
- [ ] Register for **October window** immediately upon opening — domestic seats are extremely scarce
- [ ] Confirm UTokyo score report recipient (code **7048**)
- [ ] Download and print Confirmation Letter before test day
- [ ] Verify score delivery after window closes (~8-11 days)

> [!danger] October window priority
> Mainland China has only **two test centers** (Beijing and Shanghai) `[UNVERIFIED — single test-taker report, 2025]`. Based on test-taker reports, **only the October window reliably has domestic seats** — September and April availability is unconfirmed. Do not count on a retake within the same cycle. Verify center list and seat availability on NEEA portal when the 2026-27 cycle opens.

## Exam Day Checklist (Beijing Center)

**Location:** ETS Test Center, Gongxiao International Building (宫霄国际大厦), 9F, Chaoyang District, Beijing `[UNVERIFIED — address from 2025 report; verify before test day]`.

### What to Bring

- **ID card (身份证)** — mandatory
- **Confirmation Letter (确认书)** — mandatory, print in advance
- Water bottle (for the rest area only, not allowed inside the exam room)

### What Gets Confiscated (stored in lockers)

- All bags, backpacks
- All electronic devices (phone, watch, etc.)
- Pens, paper, tissues
- **Jacket/coat** — the exam room runs hot, and jackets cannot be hung on chairs

The center provides: scratch paper, pens, tissues (ask the proctor).

### Procedure Flow `[UNVERIFIED — based on single test-taker report, Beijing center, 2025; procedure may differ by location or change between cycles]`

```
1F Entrance: ID + Confirmation Letter check → receive Exam Room Card (考场卡)
    ↓
9F Check-in: ID + Exam Room Card → sign Agreement (承诺书) → store all belongings
    ↓
Security: Empty pockets required → pass through security
    ↓
Rest Area: Store water bottle, use restroom (has tissues)
    ↓
Exam Room Door: Present ID + Exam Room Card → sign in, photo taken → escorted to seat
    ↓
Exam: 120 min, continuous timer — restroom breaks do NOT pause the clock
```

### Key Reminders

1. Arrive **30+ minutes early**
2. The clock does not stop — plan restroom breaks strategically
3. No guessing penalty — answer every question `[UNVERIFIED — confirm rights-only scoring on ETS website]`
4. Jacket rule is strictly enforced — dress in layers you can keep on

## Contingency

If the Oct 2026 window is missed or score is too low:
- **Apr 2027 window** is the next opportunity, but scores arrive ~May 2027 — this **delays application to the Oct 2028 cycle**
- No retake margin exists before the ~Dec 2026 GSGC deadline
- See [[Official_Deadlines#3. GRE Physics Subject Test]] and [[UTokyo_RIKEN]] for fallback implications
