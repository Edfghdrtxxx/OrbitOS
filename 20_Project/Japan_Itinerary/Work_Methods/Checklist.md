# Work Track Checklist


> [!warning] Verify all requirements against official sources before acting. This checklist is scaffolding, not authoritative.

## Eligibility Requirements

- [ ] Meet one legally accepted qualification route for `Engineer/Specialist in Humanities/International Services` (degree route or another recognized route under immigration criteria)
- [ ] Job offer from a Japan-based company (sponsor required)
- [ ] Planned duties and applicant background satisfy status-of-residence activity requirements

## Visa: Engineer / Specialist in Humanities / International Services

- [ ] Certificate of Eligibility (COE) - employer applies on your behalf
- [ ] Valid passport (check mission-specific validity requirements)
- [ ] Visa application at the Japanese embassy/consulate serving your place of residence
- [ ] Visa photo that matches that mission's current size/format requirements
- [ ] Supporting documents requested by mission and sponsor (commonly COE-related documents, contract/offer, CV, and academic documents)

## Arrival and Setup

- [ ] Confirm residence card issuance process at port of entry and municipality registration requirements
- [ ] Complete municipal address registration after securing housing
- [ ] Join employer social insurance or National Health Insurance, as applicable
- [ ] Open Japanese bank account after resident registration (some companies assist)
- [ ] Get a phone number (SIM / contract)
- [ ] Find housing (company housing vs. private rental)
