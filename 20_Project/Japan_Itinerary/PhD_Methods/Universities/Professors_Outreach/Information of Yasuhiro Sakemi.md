# Information of Yasuhiro Sakemi
## Quick Facts
- Position: Professor, Center for Nuclear Study (CNS), Graduate School of Science, The University of Tokyo.[^sakemi-utokyo]
- Email: sakemi@cns.s.u-tokyo.ac.jp.[^sakemi-utokyo]
- Lab: Sakemi Lab (fundamental symmetries / EDM search / laser cooling).[^sakemi-lab]
- CNS operates primarily at the RIKEN Wako campus and collaborates with RIKEN Nishina Center.[^cns-overview]

## Research Focus (public descriptions)
