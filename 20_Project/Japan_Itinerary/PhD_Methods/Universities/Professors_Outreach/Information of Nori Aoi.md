# Information of Nori Aoi
## Quick Facts
- **Position:** Professor, Center for Nuclear Study (CNS), Graduate School of Science, The University of Tokyo. [^aoi-cns]
- **Email:** aoi@cns.s.u-tokyo.ac.jp (Current); aoi@rikaxp.riken.go.jp (RIKEN). [^aoi-email]
- **Lab/Group:** NUSPEQ (NUclear SPectroscopy for Extreme Quantum system) / Exotic Nuclear Reaction Group. [^aoi-lab]
- **Primary facilities:** RIBF (RI Beam Factory) at RIKEN, SHARAQ spectrometer, OEDO beamline, CAT-M (CNS Active Target - Medium). [^aoi-research]
- **Location:** CNS is based at the RIKEN Wako campus, facilitating the JRA (Junior Research Associate) pathway. [^aoi-cns]

## Research Focus (public descriptions)
- **Exotic Nuclei:** Investigation of the structure and dynamics of nuclei far from the stability line (neutron-rich/proton-rich). [^aoi-research]
- **Gamma-Ray Spectroscopy:** Leading the use of CNS GRAPE (Gamma-Ray detector Array with Position and Energy sensitivity). [^aoi-lab]
- **Active Target TPCs:** Heavy involvement in developing and using Active Target TPCs like **CAT-M** to measure low-energy recoil particles in exotic nuclear reactions. [^aoi-catm]
- **RI Beam Experiments:** Utilizing high-intensity RI beams at RIKEN RIBF for nuclear spectroscopy and reaction studies. [^aoi-research]

## RIKEN / Facility Context
- Professor Aoi recently moved from Osaka University (RCNP) to UTokyo CNS in March 2025. [^aoi-move]
- His research is deeply integrated with the RIKEN Nishina Center, where he conducts experiments using RIBF. [^aoi-cns]
- The CAT-M project is a flagship TPC development at CNS, designed for reaction studies with RI beams. [^aoi-catm]

## Recent Publications (Key Themes)
- Studies on shell evolution and collective motions in neutron-rich nuclei. [^aoi-research]
- Development and performance validation of Active Target TPCs (CAT-M) for low-energy reaction measurements. [^aoi-catm]
- Nuclear spectroscopy of exotic isotopes using RIBF and GRAPE. [^aoi-lab]

## Student Supervision / International Students
- Previously a Professor at the Research Center for Nuclear Physics (RCNP), Osaka University. [^aoi-move]
- Now leading the NUSPEQ group at CNS, UTokyo. International students are encouraged to apply via the UTokyo Graduate School of Science. [^aoi-cns]
